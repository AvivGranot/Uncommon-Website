About

Uncommonness entered my life in 2022. It all began when I experienced the best growth in my career and life, yet felt that it didn't mean a thing to the greater world—more importantly, it didn't create any true value in global markets or for generations to come. After many conversations with friends, mentors, and peers, they encouraged me to finally write down my beliefs. I hope they weren't wrong. Please enjoy the reading, and I would be more than happy to hear your feedback and perspectives.

Goals

About Me

My name is Aviv, and I'm currenly a BA student at Reichman University. I started my path in the real estate field, growing from no knowledge at all, to be an intermediary of transactions worth tens million dollars in just three years. Yet, now I pursue greater goals, as this blog describes. To truly better the world in generations to come. Therefore I deepply seek to work with brilliant minds, values-led, and to experience over and over again 'cognitive friction'.

In my free time, I love to read books, especialy biographies of Uncommon People, to excercise, to volunteer (also founded serval programs in my university), and to just enjoy life.
